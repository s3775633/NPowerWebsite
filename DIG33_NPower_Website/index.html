About Us

Our Mission

Our core focus is to provide decentralised, green, and interconnected solutions that address the needs of our customers and those of the environment. We aim to lead the global shift towards new technology by working with customers, companies and across communities to make energy simpler, smarter and more sustainable.

Your Involvement

At npower, we believe in empowering the individual to make global change. We aim to connect our customers to our tried-and-true tips for saving money, electricity, and in turn, the planet. Whether through offering 100% renewable energy tariffs, keeping our customers updated on the latest green technology, or by educating households on how they can minimise not just their energy bills but their carbon footprint, we’re always looking for ways to do some good together.
